Section 1

Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites and web applications. It involves a range of tasks, from the user-facing front-end (what a user sees and interacts with) to the server-side back-end (the behind-the-scenes logic and databases). Key skills include programming languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as an understanding of design, database management, and performance optimization.
